Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about our rafting business, including services offered, safety guidelines, and booking options. It aims to attract adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts looking for thrilling rafting experiences.

Audience

Our target audience includes:

  • Adventure enthusiasts looking for outdoor activities.
  • Families and groups planning a fun and safe rafting trip.
  • Corporate groups interested in team-building activities.

Scenario Questions:

  1. Are you looking for an exciting outdoor activity for the weekend?
  2. Do you want a safe and fun experience for your family or group?
  3. Are you planning a corporate event with team-building activities?
